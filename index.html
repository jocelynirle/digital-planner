Download Instructions

Step 1

For this digital planner to work as intended, you will need a PDF annotation application and a tablet, though it’s possible to use with a smart phone as well. For iPad, I recommend Notability or GoodNotes, and for Android, I recommend Xodo.

Once you have a PDF annotation app installed, click the download link using your tablet or device.

Download

Step 2

You’ll be taken to the Grace Hopper Digital Planner PDF.

Click the menu in the top right corner (looks like three dots). From this menu, select, “Open in.”

Step 3

Now you have two choices. You can either download the PDF to your tablet by clicking, “Save to Files,” or you can open it directly with your PDF annotation app.

If you decide to open it with your PDF annotation app, skip to step 5.

Step 4

If you downloaded the digital planner to your files, then locate the planner in your files and open it. Click on the export icon in the top right corner, which will open a menu. Then select the app you’d like to open the digital planner with.

Step 5

In Notability and GoodNotes, you’ll be given the option to add the digital planner PDF to an existing note, or to create a new note. I recommend creating a new note.
